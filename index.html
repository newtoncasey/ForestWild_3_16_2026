Who We Are

Grounded in the Southern Forest

ForestWild Consultants LLC is a professional forestry and wildlife consulting firm headquartered in Florence, Alabama. We bring hands-on expertise in timber management, wildlife habitat enhancement, and land stewardship across the hardwood and pine forests of the Southeast.

“Across my career I’ve managed properties from 20 acres to 640,000 acres — family farms, institutional timberland, trust properties, military land. Every one of them was different, and every landowner had different goals. Our approach has never changed: we look through your lens, not ours. We determine what your land needs to achieve your vision — and where that vision is still taking shape, we help bring it into focus.”
“Before I ever walked a timber tract professionally, I served as a U.S. Army light infantry officer, deploying to Iraq and Afghanistan and spending most of my service in the 10th Mountain Division. That time in uniform shaped my professional life more than anything I could ever imagine. The values I carried home — duty, integrity, selfless service — are the same ones I bring to every landowner I work with today. I do my best to live the Ranger Creed in everything I do, and a firm faith in God leads me in all of it. I don’t claim to be the best at what I do — but I vow to work hard, think carefully, and be honest with you every step of the way.”

What We Do

Forest & Wildlife Services

🌲

Forest Management Planning

Comprehensive forest management plans tailored to your land’s species composition, soil types, and ownership goals.

🔥

Prescribed Fire & TSI

Prescribed burning plans and timber stand improvement treatments to reduce fuels, control competition, and improve wildlife habitat.

🌿

Reforestation & Site Prep

Seedling procurement, planting supervision, site preparation, and post-planting monitoring for pine, hardwood, and native species.

🪓

Timber Sales

Timber markets across the Southeast have been challenging and unpredictable — and we won’t pretend otherwise. Every tract is different. Species, volume, product mix, and location all determine whether the timing is right for a sale.

Our job is to give you an honest assessment. If your property is ready, we’ll go get you the best price the market will offer. If the timing isn’t right, we’ll be straight with you and put a plan in place to build value for a stronger sale down the road.

What we won’t do is push you into a sale that doesn’t serve your interests.

A well-managed loblolly pine stand ready for its next chapter

🦌

Wildlife Habitat Enhancement

Food plot design, habitat structure improvements, and prescribed management for white-tailed deer, turkey, quail, waterfowl, and non-game species.

🏕

Hunting Leases & Leasing Services

Boundary & Inventory Services
