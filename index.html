What We Do

Forest & Wildlife Services

🌲

Forest Management Planning

Comprehensive forest management plans tailored to your land’s species composition, soil types, and ownership goals.

🔥

Prescribed Fire & TSI

Prescribed burning plans and timber stand improvement treatments to reduce fuels, control competition, and improve wildlife habitat.

🌿

Reforestation & Site Prep

🪓

Timber Sales

Timber markets across the Southeast have been challenging and unpredictable — and we won’t pretend otherwise. Every tract is different. Species, volume, product mix, and location all determine whether the timing is right for a sale.

Our job is to give you an honest assessment. If your property is ready, we’ll go get you the best price the market will offer. If the timing isn’t right, we’ll be straight with you and put a plan in place to build value for a stronger sale down the road. What we won’t do is push you into a sale that doesn’t serve your interests.

Ask us about a marked hardwood sale. A “hardwood select” harvest means different things to different people — we’d be glad to discuss the success we’ve had with marking stands of hardwood timber and the satisfaction of the landowners involved. We’re also having success with pine sales. In these difficult markets, we have to be strategic in how and where we sell timber. Call us to discuss your objectives and let us see if we can help you be successful.

A well-managed loblolly pine stand ready for its next chapter

Wildlife Management Surveys

Camera surveys and thermal drone surveys to assess wildlife populations, activity patterns, and habitat use — giving you the data needed to make informed management decisions for deer, turkey, and other species on your property.

🦌

Wildlife Habitat Enhancement

Food plot design, habitat structure improvements, and prescribed management for white-tailed deer, turkey, quail, waterfowl, and non-game species.

Boundary & Inventory Services
